FootPick is a website that I made because my friends and I were always playing the same matches in fifa. we needed an app that could give us two random teams but still be a fair match, and that's where Footpick was born.

This project is a simple console app that gives you a random strong password containing numbers, letters and symbols. It also stores these passwords with a key which you can name whatever you want and if you forget the name you can look up all the names.

This project was the first one that I did, it's made in c# and was made to help my mother in her work. The porpuse of the app is to add clients to a database, every client has a unique id, and you can leave notes about each client to remember some details of the last visit. I later changed some small details and my stepfather also used the app.
